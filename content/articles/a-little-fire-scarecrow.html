A Little Fire Scarecrow was a recording project featuring percussion by Jeremy Green and contributions from Kevin Chatham (bass), Frederick Wells (bass), and Alva Snædís (guitar and bass).

  • Tower, a one-chord instrumental inspired by Swans. This song comes from Arcana, an unfinished 2020 concept album based on the Tarot. I absolutely love the slow build.

  • Sublimation (2019)

    A very personal album the explores my relationship with mental health challenges, including Bipolar Disorder, suicidal ideation, dissociation, and anxiety. Lacunescent is one of my favorite tracks on this album, inspired by a word I made up in a dream.

  • Scare You (2018)

    Songs and soundscapes drawn from my own dreams and nightmares. I'm particularly proud of A Shape Amongst the Stones and If Only Tonight We Could Wake, which I wrote as a sequel to one of my favorite Cure songs.

  • Cover Me and Let Me Dream (2017)

    Cover Me and Let Me Dream is the third release from A Little Fire Scarecrow. It consists of covers of songs from a variety of genres and reaches back through musical history from the 1930s to present day: from Leadbelly to Pink Floyd, Johnny Cash to Nick Cave, and Oingo Boingo to The The. Be sure to give a listen to Gloomy Sunday (The Hungarian Suicide Song) and Dead Man's Party.
