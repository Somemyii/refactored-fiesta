+++ title = "Auntie Kismet" slug = "auntie-kismet" +++

A collaboration with composer and experimental musician Craig Chin. The name is a reference to the band we were in together in the 90s, Uncle Karma.

Craig and I take turns adding or removing layers to compose these pieces. I have a one-take rule: I can either accept my first take for a given instrument, or throw it away. No do-overs!

You can listen to Auntie Kismet on Bandcamp.
