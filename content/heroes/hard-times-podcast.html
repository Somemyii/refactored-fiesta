I got my very first music review on 2021-12-14, on the Hard Times 'Fanboys' Podcast! Starting around the 27:23 mark, they talk about "The Punk", my experimental piece from this year's Drone Day festival.

  • "It made me feel a way that drone music never has, that was really really cool"
  • "It could be in the soundtrack for Prometheus"
  • "It's creepy, it's haunted, but also a little bit uplifting. You feel wonder."
  • "It's 99% of the way there, just that high end was brutal. Roll off some of the piercing high frequency; it takes you out of the sense of wonder"

I can't thank @REALpunknews enough for the kind words and constructive feedback. You can listen to the song itself on Soundcloud.
