On 2021-08-29 I was interviewed by David J Hansen for "Engaging Conversations", a series of interviews with Industrial/Goth/Post-Punk DJs and artists.

We had a great talk, covering topics like the creative process, world-building with music, mental health, and the power of storytelling.

The hardest question he asked me was, "What does music mean to you?"
