On 2021-05-09 I took part in Drone Day, a 24-hour, global celebration of drone, tone, soundscaping, and more. Streaming live for 2 hours, I created a song called "The Punk", inspired by an Irish talk show from 1982. It was a great opportunity to use my Stargazer drone synth. Something, from nothing, to something.
